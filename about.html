About Me

Who am I?

My name is Kya, and I'm a German Shepherd Dog and Belgian Malinois mix. I was born on January 8, 2023 in North Carolina. One of my ears is down, so I only hear 50% of anything that is said to me. When I'm focused, I can do lots of tricks, like sit, stay, and, my favorite, hug.

My Family

I have two dads who are the best! I also have a brother named Jackson who is a Husky Boxer mix. He can be annoying sometimes, but he's my best friend in the whole world. I also like to go play with my Auntie Bella who's a Labradoodle.

My Dreams
